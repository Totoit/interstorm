Payment method

You may be offered one or more of the following payment options:

  • Credit card (Visa, MasterCard, Diners Club, American Express)
  • Debit Card (Maestro in the UK and Carte Bleue in France)
  • PayPal (currently available for bookings in Euros, US Dollars, AU Dollars and GB Pounds only)
  • Real-time or online bank transfer
  • Western Union
  • Offline bank transfer

Since we have different payment options from different countries, we would ask that you check during the transfer process what payment options are given to you. If your desired payment option is not shown, it may not be available for the country that you are departing from, or there is too little time left before departure.

In some countries we may only offer the option to make the booking online and then make payment at an Aurum palace office.
